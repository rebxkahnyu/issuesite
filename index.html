The K-Pop Industry Isn't as Pretty as K-pop

Some Darker Truths

Rebekah Lee
3 Page Issue Site

The globalization of K-Pop has been a weird yet very cool journey. It's played a huge part in helping me recognize my own identity, and has brought good to the world. Yes, the celebrities are talented and hot, but the industry itself and its affects on consumers (us) has also brought harmful issues. Idols experience bad treatment from companies and fans, while we stay uneducated about gender and race issues. To the right are the sources used and articles about this topic, or feel free to travel to "Idols" or "Consumers" for a more general overview about why K-Pop isn't as pretty as it can seem.
